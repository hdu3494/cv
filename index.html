Huizhong Du

Senior Visual Designer at Valtech

Huizhong holds a BFA(Hons.) in Visual Communication from Nanyang Technological University. Before that, she graduated as valedictorian with a Diploma in Multimedia & Animation from Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

Currently,she’s working as a Senior Visual Designer for Valtech, a global IT service and consulting company focused on business transformation. She is specialised in UI/UX design and brand identity design.

Her experience includes working agency side on projects spanning commerce, education, health, media, and government industries and sectors for clients including L’Oréal, Tefal, NUS, Wellchem Pharmaceuticals, CinemaWorld, and TransitLink.
